About Us

This pet clinic offers all kinds of pet related services and facilities for dogs cats & birds .It is a complete care hospital for your loved ones. Mobile clinical services in Lucknow and other nearby cities .

Canine & Feline Services

Treatment ,Surgery, Vaccination of canines. General and preventive surgical procedures like neutering, castration , and other major and minor surgeries.Grooming and other hygiene services like ear and dental cleaning , nail trimming etc.

Bovine services

We provide artificial insemmination, treatment of reproductive, digestive,lactation disorders and general diseases. All thses services are provoded onsite. Consultation via video call also available.

Avian services

Treatment of diseases of various exotic birds, their prevention , minor surgeries and trauma.Consultation on video conferencing
